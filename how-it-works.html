How It Works

Simple system. Clear targets. Fast execution.

Odeology is built so someone can go from no plan to actionable calories, macros, and grocery guidance in about a minute.

1. Pick your goal and training style

You choose cut, bulk, recomp, or strength and match it to your real training style and weekly frequency.

2. Enter your body data

Height, current weight, goal weight, and session intensity let the calculator estimate your daily calorie target.

3. Get daily macro targets

You receive daily protein, carbs, and fats plus a practical note so you know exactly what to execute.

4. Turn numbers into groceries

After macros are unlocked, the grocery flow translates those numbers into affordable foods and portions.

5. Review and adjust weekly

As your weight trend changes, targets can be adjusted to keep progress moving in the right direction.
